Wave equation

Construct wave equation

Vertical displacement function: \(u(x,t)\)

Mass density function: \(\rho_{0}(x)\)

Tension function: \(T(x,t)\)

Angle function: \(\theta(x,t)\)

Definition: Perfectly elastic string

Perfectly elastic string has the tension \(T(x,t)\) is a constant \(T_{0}\).

Definition: Perfectly flexible string

The force exert on every segment of perfectly flexible string is tangential, such tangential force is call tension.

Let's try to derive the one-dimensional wave equation with the functions above.

\[ \text{slope of the segment} = \dfrac{\partial u}{\partial x} = \tan \theta(x, t) = \dfrac{\sin \theta(x, t)}{\cos \theta(x, t)} \approx \sin \theta(x, t) \approx \theta(x, t). \]
