02 Introduction to limits

Posted on 2021-10-14 Edited on 2021-11-09 In 课程（Lecture） , 爱荷华州立（ISU） , 数学（Math） Disqus:
  • [[Slope]] \[\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}\]
  • [[Limit]] \[\lim _{x \rightarrow a} f(x)=L\]
  • [[Limit|Basic properties of limits]] \(\lim _{x \rightarrow a} f(x)=L\) and \(\lim _{x \rightarrow a} g(x)=M\)
    • \(\lim _{x \rightarrow a}(c f(x)+d g(x))=c L+d M\)
    • \(\lim _{x \rightarrow a}(f(x) g(x))=L M\)
    • \(M \neq 0, \lim _{x \rightarrow a} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)}=\frac{L}{M}\)
    • \(L>0\), \(\lim _{x \rightarrow a}(f(x))^{q}=L^{q}\)
    • Polynomial of limit \[\begin{aligned} \lim _{x \rightarrow a} f(x) &=\lim _{x \rightarrow a}\left(c_{n} x^{n}+\cdots+c_{1} x+c_{0}\right) \\ &=c_{n}\left(\lim _{x \rightarrow a} x^{n}\right)+\cdots+c_{1}\left(\lim _{x \rightarrow a} x\right)+c_{0}\left(\lim _{x \rightarrow a} 1\right) \\ &=c_{n}\left(\lim _{x \rightarrow a} x\right)^{n}+\cdots+c_{1}\left(\lim _{x \rightarrow a} x\right)+c_{0}\left(\lim _{x \rightarrow a} 1\right) \\ &=c_{n} a^{n}+\cdots+c_{1} a+c_{0}=f(a) \end{aligned}\]
  • Rational Function \[\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\]
  • \(\frac{0}{0}\)? What to do?
    • Expanding and factoring polynomial
    • Multiplying by the conjugate
      • \((a+b)(a-b)=a^{2}-b^{2} \to \sqrt{some thing}\)
    • Use [[Triangle Identity|trig identity]]
      • \(\sin ^{2} \theta+\cos ^{2} \theta=1\)
