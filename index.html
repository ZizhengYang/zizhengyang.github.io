#6 Postulate

The time evolution of a quantum system is determined by the Hamiltonian or total [[Hamiltonian operator|energy operator]] $H(t)$ through the Schrödinger equation:
$$
i \hbar \frac{d}{d t}|\psi(t)\rangle=\hat{H}(t)|\psi(t)\rangle
$$

  • observable -> Hermitian
  • Eigenvalues: allowed energies
  • Eigenvectors: complete basis
  • Eigenstates: $\hat{H}\left|E_{n}\right\rangle=E_{n}\left|E_{n}\right\rangle$
  • General statevectors: $|\psi(t)\rangle=\sum_{n} c_{n}(t)\left|E_{n}\right\rangle$
    • $|\psi(t)\rangle$: time evolved state
    • $c_{n}(t)$: time dependence live in coefficient
    • $\left|E_{n}\right\rangle$: energy basis
Derive Schrödinger Time Evolution
  • For $i \hbar \frac{d}{d t}|\psi(t)\rangle=\hat{H}(t)|\psi(t)\rangle$
    • substitute with $|\psi(t)\rangle=\sum_{n} c_{n}(t)\left|E_{n}\right\rangle$
  • $i \hbar \frac{d}{d t}\left(\sum_{n} c_{n}(t)\left|E_{n}\right\rangle\right)=\widehat{H}\left(\sum_{n} c_{n}(t)\left|E_{n}\right\rangle\right)$
  • $i \hbar \sum_{n} \frac{d c_{n}(t)}{d t}\left|E_{n}\right\rangle=\sum_{n} c_{n}(t) \widehat{H}(t)\left|E_{n}\right\rangle$
  • take the inner product with a generic energy eigenstate
    • $\left\langle E_{k}\left|i \hbar \sum_{n} \frac{d c_{n}(t)}{d t}\right| E_{n}\right\rangle=\left\langle E_{k}\left|\sum_{n} c_{n}(t) \widehat{H}\right| E_{n}\right\rangle$
  • $i \hbar \sum_{n} \frac{d c_{n}(t)}{d t}\left\langle E_{k} \mid E_{n}\right\rangle=\sum_{n} c_{n}(t)\left\langle E_{k}|\widehat{H}(t)| E_{n}\right\rangle$
  • $i \hbar \frac{d c_{k}(t)}{d t}=E_{k} c_{k}(t)$
    • for $\left\langle E_{k} \mid E_{n}\right\rangle=1$ only when $k=n$
    • for $\left\langle E_{k}|\widehat{H}(t)| E_{n}\right\rangle=1$ only when $k=n$
  • $\frac{d c_{k}(t)}{d t}=-\left(i E_{k} / \hbar\right) c_{k}(t)$
  • $c_{k}(t)=c_{k}(0) e^{-i E_{k} t / \hbar}$ ^e6d634
    • solution to a 1st order DE
  • At $t=0$
    • $|\psi(t=0)\rangle=\sum_{n} c_{n}(0)\left|E_{n}\right\rangle$
      • substitute with $c_{k}(t)=c_{k}(0) e^{-i E_{k} t / \hbar}$ [[#^e6d634]]
  • $|\psi(t)\rangle=\sum_{n} c_{n}(0) e^{-i E_{n} t / \hbar}\left|E_{n}\right\rangle$
Schrödinger Time Evolution

$$|\psi(t)\rangle=\sum_{n} c_{n}(0) e^{-i E_{n} t / \hbar}\left|E_{n}\right\rangle$$

Time-independent 2-state system (with no superposition by t=0)
  • $|\psi(t=0)\rangle=\left|E_{1}\right\rangle$
  • $|\psi(t)\rangle=e^{-i E_{1} t / \hbar}\left|E_{1}\right\rangle$
  • $\wp=\left|\left\langle a_{1} \mid \psi(t)\right\rangle\right|^{2}=\left|\left\langle a_{1}\left|e^{-i E_{1} t / \hbar}\right| E_{1}\right\rangle\right|^{2}=\left|\left\langle a_{1} \mid E_{1}\right\rangle\right|^{2}$
Time-independent 2-state system (with superposition by t=0)
  • $|\psi(t=0)\rangle=c_{1}\left|E_{1}\right\rangle+c_{2}\left|E_{2}\right\rangle$
  • $|\psi(t)\rangle=c_{1} e^{-i E_{1} t / \hbar}\left|E_{1}\right\rangle+c_{2} e^{-i E_{2} t / \hbar}\left|E_{2}\right\rangle$
  • $\wp=\left|\left\langle E_{1} \mid \psi(t)\right\rangle\right|^{2}=\mid\left\langle E_{1}\left|\left(c_{1} e^{-i E_{1} t / \hbar}\left|E_{1}\right\rangle+c_{2} e^{-i E_{2} t /\hbar}\left|E_{2}\right\rangle\right)\right|^{2}\right.=\left|c_{1}\right|^{2}$
  • For any other observable $A\left|a_{1}\right\rangle=a_{1}\left|a_{1}\right\rangle$
    • $[A,H]=0$
    • $A$ and $H$ must share an eigen-basis
    • $\left|a_{1}\right\rangle$ is an energy eigenstate
    • probability of measuring eigenvalue $a_{1}$ depends on time
